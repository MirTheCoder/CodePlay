Pump It Up

What is Pump It Up

This artwork is a piece that I made with the intent of providing a vibrant and party like vibe. It kind of is like teh school party events or community events you wen to where they might have glow sticks and people just dancing. This was the kind of vibe that I was going for and wanted to put in a boombox dj to really emphasizes the music take over nad just make the vibes that more surreal. I added a lot of lights in order to make the artwork seem that more electric and make the vibes that electric as well. I wanted to make it like as if a person could feel like they are in there as well and its just suppose to overall produce a party and vibrant culture

What was the inspiration

There were actually a couple inspirations that contributed to teh creation of this piece of art. For starters, the beat that I made to this was actually based off or made based off of one of those reel beats that I have heard. I liked how it sounded and tried to create something similar but with my own spin off. Then the thoughts of a party and the DJ events that I have been at start to flow into my head, and I was like "Lets do it". It gave a very upbeat and electric thought in my head. But not, for the boom box, I got inspiration from a kids show called the amazing world of gumball. I used to watch that show a lot of growing up and the boom box character named juke was meant to be a human character with a boom box for a head and I thought that would be perfect for a DJ party event. And with that, the art wok PUMP IT UP was born 😄

How Long did it take

This image probably took like two or three weeks to finish, I don't really remember exactly the length of time that it took. I do however remember that I spent some time with lining up the boom box with the body to ensure that it looked like a living boom box without the body seeming to unorthodox. I was trying to just kind of match the boom box character in the amazing world of gumball to be honest. I also do remember the turntable taking a while to create as well. The detail and constant having to group and ungroup shapes was a little tedious, but we finally made it and got it done. #We did it 😮‍💨
