What is Retro

This is retro and it is an arcade game like structure featuring ghost from Pac man
and the spaceship from galga in a space like environment with a touch of some twinkiling stars and just a pretty cool vibe in general as well. I also made a sci fi retro game like beat to go with it as well

What was the inspiration


This artwork was inspired by an arcade like mindest. I wanted to give a more outer space kind of vibe and do a little throwback to back in the day when people played at the arcade games such as pac man and galga, and I thought it would be cool to make a mashup of them and bring both worlds together, kind of putting the viewer into an errie world where they are in the small spaceship surrounded by ghost just like the ones Pac man had to face
